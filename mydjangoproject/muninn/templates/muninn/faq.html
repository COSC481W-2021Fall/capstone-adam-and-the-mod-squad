FAQ

Dashboard

Making a task:

Pick a desired list.

Enter a task into text bar. Please do not leave it empty.

Press add task.

Note that Tasks get deleted at the end of the day. Habits stay in the list.

Completing a task:

Click a task. Once it changes to orange it is complete.

Deleting a task:

Press delete button next to task.

Editing a task:

Press edit button next to task.

Change information in text box.

Press save.

Other:

Points are awarded at the end of each day. Level updates once you finish a task.

Petshop

Buying pets:

Pick a pet that you are the right level for and have enough money for.

You can not buy a pet that you cannot afford or have enough money for.

Press buy button.

Pick a name.

If you don't want the pet either click the x in the naming area or click anywhere on the page.

Naming pets:

Enter a name in the prompt after pressing the buy button.

Press buy pet.

Roost

Finding a pet:

Find the search bar on page (above all the pets) or press the filter drop down menu.

Enter name or animal.

Seeing a bigger version of the pet:

Click on pet. On the top part of the page you will see a bigger version of the pet.
